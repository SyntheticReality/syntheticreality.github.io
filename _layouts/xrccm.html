△ Syry is a leading VR/AR company at the intersection of gaming, simulations, and education. We are backed by +7 years of experience and a portfolio of +50 projects for global clients.

We specialize in developing cutting-edge projects for clients and building our own proprietary apps and video games. As the demand for immersive projects continues to grow, we invite you to join us on this journey to shape the future of spatial computing!

Role and Responsibilities

We're looking for an XR Content & Community Manager to enhance our brand presence and engage our community through captivating XR content and strategic social media management.

You will be at the forefront of creating innovative videos, reels, use cases, articles, and walkthroughs that showcase the magic of our XR projects and applications to the world.

As a Content Creator, you’ll harness the power of XR to produce content that highlights our technology's potential, crafting stories that resonate with our audience and elevate our brand. You will also use our proprietary application to create fun and engaging content at scale.

As a Social Media Manager, your role will extend to curating and sharing this content across our social media platforms, engaging with our community, and amplifying our presence in the digital world.

Project Collaboration and Deliverables

In this freelance role, we value your expertise and creativity. We are committed to defining project deliverables collaboratively, based on your input and our strategic goals. This may include, but is not limited to, producing a specific number of videos, posts, articles, and reels. We aim to tailor these deliverables to leverage your strengths and meet our audience's needs.
