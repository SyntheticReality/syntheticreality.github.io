Chief Technology Officer


At Syry, we’re on a mission to accelerate the future towards global, high-quality education, and we’re looking for a co-founder CTO to help us lead the charge.

About Us

Founded in 2023 by XR veterans, Syry is built on the foundation of a track record of +7 years in the XR sector and +50 projects delivered to global companies.

Our mission is fueled by a grant from StartUp Peru and strategic partnerships with notable accelerators like Startup Wise Guys in Europe and the Institute for the Future of Education.

Our innovative platform, with the MVP already captivating audiences, uses VR, AI, and virtual avatars to tackle the content creation challenge, a significant barrier to the widespread adoption of immersive education.




Our Vision

Traditional online learning has significant challenges, notably poor engagement and low completion rates (5-15%). VR learning has emerged as a promising alternative with greater immersion and effectiveness. Yet, its adoption is restricted by complex production processes and prohibitive costs.

Now imagine a world where educators can instantly transport students to the heart of a bustling ancient city, the serene expanse of a distant galaxy, or the intricate workings of a human cell. This is the world Syry is building - an ultimate virtual classroom that redefines the boundaries of education.

By merging XR with AI, we empower educators with endless resources to craft engaging lessons—all without the need for coding or complex tools.
