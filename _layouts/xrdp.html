Role and Responsibilities

We're looking for an XR Design & Project Lead for project-specific collaboration.

As a Project Manager, you will be assigned the responsibility of managing a project's execution, from conception to completion, within time and budget, ensuring exceptional client and internal team satisfaction.

You will work in a squad consisting of a developer and a 3D artist, reporting to the project’s executive sponsor. We will be expecting high levels of independence, reliable delegation of tasks, autonomy in action, and a self-starter with a positive driven spirit.
